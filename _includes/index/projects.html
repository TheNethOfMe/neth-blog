Honest Piranha

This is a landing page I created for my YouTube channel and could be easily adapted to other channels. It can also be used to display blog entries and podcasts. It allows users to log-in and leave comments with a robust moderation system that allows administrators to approve comments before they are displayed. I created a custom admin area that allows administartors to add new entries and series without needing to touch the code.

My 90s Notebook

My idea for a social media site with a heavy 90s asthetic. The site allows users to create an account and leave up to six publicly available posts at a time. This was a purposeful decision, as my philosophy behind social media is that posts shouldn't be perminant and that we should be forced to be selective in what we choose to share. I think a system like this could prevent the sort of overload we get from modern social media sites. I plan to update this with more features and perhaps a game.
