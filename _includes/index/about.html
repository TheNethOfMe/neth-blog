About Me

I’m a guy who likes to walk the line between designer and developer. I like to work in the front-end to make eye-catching designs that anyone can use and in the back-end to turn simple pages into fully functional web applications.

I currently live in Kent with my amazing wife, Ellen, and our two adorable cats.

A career in tech means always learning what’s new. So when I’m not acquiring new skills or putting my existing ones to work, I enjoy making podcasts and videos.

I’m passionate about social justice, retro tech and toys, games, animation, cooking and way too many other things to list here!
